WHO IS R.O.B ?

Created in 1985 by Nintendo, R.O.B (Robotic Operating Buddy) was a toy accesory for the NES. Despite beeing a commercial failure, he became later on a playable character in games such as Mario kart and most notably, the Super Smash Bros. series

In the game Super smash Bros. Ultimate, R.O.B is considered as a "broken character" that can do "everything at once" and "polyvalent". That is why, R.O.B industry and Nintendo have been working very hard to make this a reality.

Planned for 2024, Our first prototype would mean a lot for the world and the robotic industry. This Robot will be connected to your smartphone and will be "polyvalent" as it is in the game super smash bros. ultimate. Its design will remain the same as the toy but you will able to custom the color of your R.O.B and give it a name

Cooking,Helping people,Protect,Fly,Cleaning,Carrying... R.O.B will be the Robot for everyone. At home or at work.

Check the details just below!

Connected to your smartphone

Where ever you are, at any time, you can control R.O.B with your smartphone to make any action you desire with the "R.O.B CO" app.

With the "auto" mod, you also can programm a schedual for anything you want: cooking food, take out your dog...

Carries a lot

Need someone to carry your heavy bags? No problem.

It doesn't matter if you are someone going to a meeting with a heavy suitcase or if you are someone that works with heavy block of concretes. With his long arms made of steel and carbon fiber, R.O.B can carry up to 500kg.

He can also take care of some hazardous materials and more...

Awareness

R.O.B is able to recognize human feelings and can help people in need.

He can call an ambulance, take care of someone and even extinguish a fire

Cooks really well

R.O.B can prepare and cook some awesome food.

You want some pizza or some sushi home made? He will do it for you as long he gets the ingredients.

Need a vaccum cleaner?

R.O.B can clean all sorts of places, including humanly difficult places such as really narrow or high aereas.

Much more (coffee included)

R.O.B can obviously do more than that... He can save lives but also prepare a cup of coffee

He won't get bored!
